chef's & special

Our chef's special Japanese ramen bowl with beef is a flavorful and satisfying dish featuring tender slices of beef simmered in a rich, savory broth. The ramen noodles are perfectly cooked, and the bowl is garnished with fresh vegetables, scallions, and a soft-boiled egg. This special creation combines traditional Japanese flavors with a hearty twist, offering a delightful and comforting meal. Each bowl is crafted with care to deliver an authentic and memorable dining experience, showcasing the skill and creativity of our chefs.

Our chef's special, Japanese Sizzler Rice Bowl, features tender beef, crisp vegetables, and fragrant rice. Served sizzling hot, this dish combines juicy beef with a medley of vegetables, creating a delicious and dynamic meal. The sizzle adds a delightful texture and aroma, making each bite a flavorful experience. Perfect for those seeking a hearty, satisfying, and authentic Japanese dining experience.

about nihon nook

Nihon Nook ensures a seamless food delivery experience by offering an intuitive app interface for easy ordering. Customers browse a curated menu of authentic Japanese dishes, place their order, and receive real-time updates on delivery status. Orders are carefully prepared by skilled chefs and dispatched by reliable delivery partners, ensuring timely and fresh arrival. The app provides features like order tracking, customizable preferences, and customer support to enhance satisfaction and convenience.
